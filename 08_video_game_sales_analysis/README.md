# Video Game Sales Analysis

### Description
This project analyzes global video game sales data from 1980 to 2020 using Python and its libraries.

### Dataset
* **Name:** Video Game Sales 
* **Source:** [Kaggle - Video Game Sales](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/gregorut/videogamesales)
* **File Used:** `vgsales.csv`

### Tasks Performed
* Ranked the Top 10 best-selling individual games globally.
* Grouped 12+ genres into a "Top 7 + Others" category for market share analysis.
* Converted years into DateTime objects to track total sales trends over 40 years.
* Calculated and ranked total sales per hardware platform (PS2, X360, etc.).
* Implemented custom data labeling to show exact sales figures on bar charts.

### Key Findings
* **Action Genre Dominance:** The Action genre is the undisputed market leader, accounting for nearly **19.6%** of all global sales.
* **The 2008 Industry Peak:** Global physical game sales reached their absolute peak between **2008 and 2009**, followed by a steady decline as the industry shifted toward digital storefronts.
* **PlayStation Legacy:** The **PS2** remains the highest-selling hardware platform in history with over **1.2 Billion** total copies sold.
* **Market Concentration:** Action, Sports, and Shooters together represent nearly **half** of the entire historical gaming market.
